## What Is Katalist AI, Actually?

Katalist is a web-based AI storyboard platform founded in 2023 by Blaž Blokar (CTO) and Andraž Zvonar (CEO), two guys who quit their day jobs to build what they call a "translation layer" between your creative ideas and generative AI. Their target audience: filmmakers, advertisers, content creators, educators, and anyone who regularly needs to visualize a story before shooting or publishing.

The workflow is genuinely simple. You upload your script — as a CSV, Word doc, PowerPoint, or just paste it directly — and Katalist breaks it down into shots, identifies characters and scenes, and generates a visual storyboard automatically. No prompting expertise needed. No Photoshop skills required.

What separates Katalist from a generic AI image generator is the character consistency feature. If "Nick" appears in scene 1 and scene 47, he looks like the same person in both. That sounds basic, but anyone who's tried to maintain character consistency in AI image generation knows it's anything but.

## The Features Worth Talking About

**Script-to-storyboard in one click.** The headline feature. Upload your script and the AI analyzes it for characters, scenes, and visual cues — then generates the full storyboard. One reviewer timed it: under 5 minutes from script upload to complete storyboard.

**Consistent characters across every frame.** Once you define a character, Katalist locks their visual identity throughout the entire project. Changed your mind about who's playing the lead? Swap them out across the whole storyboard with a single click.

**Character Posing Tool.** Control your character's pose directly — hand up, hand down, jumping, sitting. If the AI generates a frame with an awkward stance, you can repose without regenerating the entire shot.

**Dynamic Scene Control.** Change the setting mid-story without rebuilding everything. Same character, different world — dinosaur land, medieval castle, time machine — it handles the transitions cleanly.

**AI Video Studio.** The newer addition. Katalist can now turn your storyboard into an actual video, complete with voiceover, music, and sound effects. The video models supported include Runway Gen-4, Google Veo 3.2, Kling Pro & Standard, Pixverse v4, WAN-I2V, Sora 2, and LTX-2. Plus lip-sync.

**Adobe Premiere Pro Integration.** Available on Pro and above. Your captions, timing, and metadata export directly into a ready-made timeline — reportedly saving at least an hour per project for regular users.

**Art styles.** Cinematic (realistic 3D, good for film and ads) and Sketch (2D black-and-white, good for comics, animation pitches, children's content). Multiple aspect ratios: 16:9, 9:16, and 1:1.

**Export options.** Share as a public link, download as a ZIP, export as PowerPoint, or export as a video slideshow. Enterprise plans add Premiere Pro and Final Cut Pro export.
